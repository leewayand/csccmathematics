\begin{exercise}




Let $p(k)$ be defined as follows.

\[
p(k) = 
\begin{cases}
  k^2 + 1 & \text{ if } -7 < k \leq -4 \\
  -4k + 3 & \text{ if } -4 < k \leq 1 \\
  1 - 3k & \text{ if } k > 3
\end{cases}
\]



$p(-5) = \answer{26}$ \\


$p(-4) = \answer{17}$ \\


$p(-3) = \answer{15}$ \\


$p(-2) = \answer{11}$ \\


$p(-1) = \answer{7}$ \\


$p(0) = \answer{3}$ \\


$p(1) = \answer{-1}$ \\



$p(4) = \answer{-11}$ \\


\end{exercise}
