\end{cases}
\]

$V$ has many features and characteristics, including a global maximum and minimum.  






\begin{question}



Let $P(n) = 5 V(3n - 2) + 1$


\item $V$ has a global minimum of $-7$, which occurs at $4$.  



Corresponding to this, $P$ has a global \wordChoice{\choice[correct]{minimum} \choice{maximum}} of $\answer{-34}$, which occurs at $\answer{2}$.


\end{question}








\begin{question}



Let $P(n) = 5 V(3n - 2) + 1$


\item $V$ has a global maximum of $9$, which occurs at $-6$ and at $0$.  



Corresponding to these, $P$ has a global \wordChoice{\choice{minimum} \choice[correct]{maximum}} of $\answer{46}$, which occurs at $\answer{\frac{-4}{3}}$ and at $\answer{\frac{2}{3}}$.


\end{question}
