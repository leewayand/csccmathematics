Define $H(k)$ as 
\[
H(k) = \ln\left(  \frac{k^4 (k-1)^3}{(k+5)^2}  \right)
\]



Rewrite $H$ as a sum/difference of logarithms of linear functions



\[
H(k) = 4 \ln(k) +  3 \answer{\ln(k-1)} - \answer{2} \ln(k+5)
\]
