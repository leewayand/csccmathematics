\begin{question}

Define $f$ and $g$ by the following formulas and their natural domains. \\


\[
f(x) = 3 \, x + 5
\]



\[
g(t) = 3 \, t - 5
\]


Are $f$ and $g$ inverse functions? \\


\begin{multipleChoice}
\choice {Yes}
\choice [correct]{No}
\end{multipleChoice}

\end{question}
