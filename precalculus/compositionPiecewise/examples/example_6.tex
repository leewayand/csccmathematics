\begin{question}


To investigate the function $B \circ A$ we need the range of $A$ and the domain of $B$.


The range of $A$ is  $\left[ \answer{-4}, \answer{-2} \right] \cup \left[ \answer{2}, \answer{6} \right)$. \\



The domain of $B$ is  $\left( \answer{-6}, \answer{-2} \right) \cup \left[ \answer{3}, \answer{5} \right)$. \\


Their intersection is $\left[ \answer{-4}, \answer{-2} \right) \cup \left[ \answer{3}, \answer{5} \right)$. \\



\end{question}
