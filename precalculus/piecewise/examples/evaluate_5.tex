\begin{exercise}




Let $A(y)$ be defined as follows.

\[
A(y) = 
\begin{cases}
  3 - 2y & \text{ if } -5 < y \leq 1 \\
  (y-3)^2 & \text{ if } 3 < y \leq 9 \\
  \frac{2}{y+1} & \text{ if } 11 < y 
\end{cases}
\]


$3 A(-4) - 1 = \answer{32}$ \\


$3 - A(-1)  = \answer{-2}$ \\


$A(-2) A(4) = \answer{7}$ \\


$A(5) - A(1) = \answer{3}$ \\


$A(9-9)(A(9) - 9) = \answer{0}$ \\  


$A(-6) = \answer{DNE}$ \\


$A(2) = \answer{DNE}$ \\


$A(10) = \answer{DNE}$ \\


\end{exercise}
