\begin{question}



$V(-2) = 1$ \\


$(0.5, 1.5)$ is an open interval around $1$. \\


It is possible to pick $\delta$ small enough so that for all $x \in (-2 - \delta, -2 + \delta)$ we have $V(x) \in (0.5, 1.5)$.
