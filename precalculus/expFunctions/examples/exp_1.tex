\begin{exercise}




Let $f(x) = 7 \cdot 3^{2x+1} -5$ with its naturla domain.


\begin{question}


Determine $A$ and $R$ so that $f$ can be written in the form 
\[
f(x) = A \cdot R^x - 5
\]



\[
A = \answer{21}
\]


\[
R = \answer{9}
\]


\end{question}
