\begin{question}




Let  $f(x) = \frac{x^2 - 9}{x - 3}$.


$f$ is not defined at $3$.


Come up with a nice function $g(k)$, such that $g$ is defined at $3$ and $g = f$ everywhere besides $3$.



\[
g(k) = \answer{k+3}
\]







\end{question}
